FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about your dental treatment? We've answered the most common ones below.

Is root canal treatment painful?
Modern root canal treatment is performed under local anesthesia and is virtually painless. With advanced techniques used at Dr. Smile, most patients report feeling little to no discomfort during the procedure. You may experience mild soreness afterward, which subsides within a day or two.
How long does teeth whitening take?
Professional in-clinic teeth whitening at Dr. Smile typically takes about 45–60 minutes for a single session. Most patients see noticeable results immediately after the first treatment. For best results, we may recommend a take-home maintenance kit as well.
How often should I visit the dentist?
We recommend visiting the dentist at least twice a year (every 6 months) for routine checkups and professional cleaning. Regular visits help detect early signs of cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues before they become serious problems.
Are braces suitable for adults?
Absolutely! Braces and clear aligners are suitable for adults of all ages. At Dr. Smile, we offer both traditional metal braces and discreet clear aligners. There is no age limit for orthodontic treatment — it's never too late to achieve a straighter, more confident smile.
